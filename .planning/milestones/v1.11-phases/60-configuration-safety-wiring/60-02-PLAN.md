<objective>
Wire the grace period timer, feature-enabled gate, and config-driven weights into the steering daemon runtime. When disabled or in grace period, wan_zone is nullified before ConfidenceSignals construction (zero changes needed in scoring functions for the gate). Config-driven weights are passed to compute_confidence() via optional parameters.

Purpose: Complete the safety wiring so WAN signal is ignored for 30s after startup and when disabled, and weights are configurable rather than hardcoded class constants.
Output: Fully gated WAN awareness with config-driven weights, comprehensive tests.
