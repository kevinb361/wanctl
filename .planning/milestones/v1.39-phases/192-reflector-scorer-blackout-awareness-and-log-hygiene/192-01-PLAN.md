<objective>
Add a caller-side blackout gate in `wan_controller.py` that skips `ReflectorScorer.record_results(...)` when the current RTT cycle reports zero successful reflectors, so per-host quality windows are not decremented by stale/replayed cached data during carrier ICMP blackouts.

Purpose: Fix the cross-object bug where zero-success cycles feed previous-cycle per-host attribution back into the scorer's rolling windows, causing scores to sink toward the 0.8 deprioritize threshold during path-wide ICMP blackouts (MEAS-05, MEAS-06).

Output: `_should_skip_scorer_update()` helper on WANController, two gated call sites, and unit + seam integration tests proving the gate behaves per D-01/D-02/D-03.
</objective>
