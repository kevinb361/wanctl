<objective>
Add spike detector confirmation counter to eliminate DOCSIS cable flapping caused by single-sample RTT jitter triggering instant RED state.

Purpose: The spike detector fires ~9,225 times/hr on idle DOCSIS cable due to 15-70ms cycle-to-cycle jitter. Each false positive resets green_streak, blocking rate recovery. Requiring 3 consecutive spike cycles (150ms at 50ms interval) eliminates single-sample noise while adding negligible detection latency for real congestion.

Output: Modified spike detector with `accel_confirm_cycles` config param (default 3), `_spike_streak` counter, and full test coverage.
</objective>
