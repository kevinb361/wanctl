<objective>
Extend `_check_protocol_correlation()` in `wan_controller.py` with a fusion-aware cooldown branch that stretches the INFO-log interval for `Protocol deprioritization detected` emissions when fusion cannot act on the signal (disabled, healer None, or healer-suspended). Normal-mode cooldown is unchanged. First occurrences and real protocol-ratio transitions still log once cooldown has elapsed.

Purpose: Cut the Spectrum 2.5k/day INFO log volume (OPER-02) that appears when protocol deprioritization persists under a suspended fusion healer — without demoting the log level globally, without widening the normal cooldown, and without coupling healer transitions into the protocol latch.

Output: One new cooldown attribute on WANController (initialized at construction time alongside the existing `_irtt_deprioritization_*` triad), a fusion-state read at the log site, and unit tests covering normal-mode / suspended-mode / transition-preservation / latch-isolation / D-06 fusion-transition-does-not-mutate-latch.
</objective>
