<objective>
Fix two independent hot-loop performance issues: (1) reduce retry_with_backoff delays on RouterOS run_cmd from 1s+2s blocking to 50ms with single retry, and (2) replace time.sleep with shutdown_event.wait in the autorate main loop for instant signal responsiveness.

Purpose: The autorate hot loop runs every 50ms. Current retry delays (1s initial, 2s second attempt) block the loop for 3+ seconds on transient failures. Current time.sleep ignores signals until the sleep completes, delaying shutdown by up to one full cycle.

Output: Modified retry decorator params on both transport run_cmd methods, shutdown_event.wait in main loop sleep.
</objective>
