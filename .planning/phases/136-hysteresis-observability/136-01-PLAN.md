<objective>
Add windowed suppression rate tracking to QueueController and expose it via the health endpoint, with periodic INFO logging during congestion windows.

Purpose: Cumulative `_transitions_suppressed` counter is useless for real-time monitoring without uptime context. A windowed counter enables operators to detect when suppression rates spike during congestion, distinguishing healthy jitter filtering from excessive suppression (potential false negatives).

Output: QueueController with 60s windowed counter, health endpoint with `suppressions_per_min` field, periodic INFO log at window boundary during congestion.
</objective>
